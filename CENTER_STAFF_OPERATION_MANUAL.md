# Center Staff Operation Manual

## Daily Flow

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Review today's attendance, no-shows, renewal targets, and inactive members.
3. Use the kiosk for member self check-in.
4. Use staff check-in when a member needs help.
5. Cancel mistaken attendance from today's attendance screen and confirm remaining sessions are restored.
6. Add member notes only when they are operationally necessary.

## Kiosk

- Staff unlocks kiosk with the center PIN.
- Members search by the last 4 phone digits.
- Member-facing kiosk screens must show masked name and masked phone only.
- Member notes, audit logs, full phone numbers, and staff-only details must not appear on kiosk screens.

## Member Management

- Register members with the minimum required data.
- Keep member pass status and remaining sessions accurate.
- Use notes for center operations context, not sensitive personal history.

## Reports

- Use monthly reports for attendance volume, no-show trends, and renewal follow-up.
- Treat reports as internal center operation data.
